Research Experience

As a Bachelor of Science (Honours) Information Technology student majoring in Software Engineering at Universiti Utara Malaysia (UUM), I was invited to join a Prototype Research Grant Scheme (PRGS) project as a Research Assistant. The project focuses on developing a mobile puzzle game as a psychotherapeutic intervention for elderly individuals with memory disorders. This opportunity allowed me to apply my technical and communication skills in a real-world research environment.
